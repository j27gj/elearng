Our History

Started in 2017, E-learn quickly established itself as a good larning platform with its unique video lectures. It makes DISTANT LEARNING PROGRAMS much more effective, and makes experience just like a normal class. Lectures on any topic can be conducted by a teacher and the students desiring to undertake these lectures get connected hence forming a class.
